# Product

<!-- impeccable:product-schema 1 -->

## Platform

web

## Users
Fitness-conscious individuals, vegetarians, and vegans looking for high-protein meals. They are typically busy and need quick, tasty options that help them meet their nutritional goals.

## Product Purpose
To provide a curated collection of high-protein vegetarian and vegan recipes that are quick to prepare and don't sacrifice flavor.

## Positioning
Hot Recipes stands out by specializing purely in the intersection of high-protein and plant-based diets, with a strong emphasis on speed (many recipes are 5-15 minutes) and utility (air fryer and Ninja Creami focus).

## Operating Context
A mobile-friendly web application used in the kitchen or for meal planning. It integrates with Pinterest for social sharing and focuses on searchability through Nuxt Content.

## Capabilities and Constraints
- **Multi-language:** Full support for English and Spanish.
- **Data-driven:** Powered by Nuxt Content v3 with SQLite indexing.
- **Tech Stack:** Nuxt 4, Nuxt UI v3, and Tailwind CSS v4.

## Brand Commitments
- **Name:** Hot Recipes.
- **Voice:** Direct, encouraging, and focused on efficiency.
- **Domain:** hotrecipes.co.uk.
- **Assets:** Pinterest presence at `hotRecipesUk`.

## Evidence on Hand
- Nearly 100 high-protein recipes already digitized in `content/recipes/`.
- Established i18n structure for ES/EN.
- Consistent metadata format including protein counts and prep times.

## Product Principles
- **Flavor First:** No sacrifices in taste for the sake of macros.
- **Clarity and Speed:** Recipes must be easy to read and fast to execute.
- **Plant-Powered:** High protein does not require meat.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility (A11y) expected for high-contrast reading in kitchen environments.

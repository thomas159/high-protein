- question: What can I use instead of tamarind paste?

steps:
- Get your coconut milk and red curry paste in a saucepan over low-medium heat. Stir
  gently. Cook until fragrant and small beads of red oil shimmer on top. This means
  the spices have officially bloomed.
- Stir in the chopped peanuts. Cook for 2 minutes. The sauce will tighten up into
  a thick, glossy glaze.
- Kill the heat entirely. Stir in the sugar and tamarind paste. Boiling tamarind destroys
  the bright, tangy notes and turns the sauce bitter, so keep it strictly off the
  heat.

faq:
- question: Do I have to use Xanthan gum?
  answer: No. The protein powder and natural apple fruit sugars provide enough structure. Add a pinch if you really want, but it's completely unnecessary.
- question: Can I increase the honey for a carb refeed?
  answer: Absolutely. If you want a solid, fast-digesting carb spike to restock your energy stores after a brutal training session, scale up the honey.
- question: Can I just blend raw apple?
  answer: You can, but microwaving the apple with the cinnamon first softens the flesh and builds a deep, jammy flavour that replicates a proper British pudding.
steps:
- Dice the apple. Toss it into a microwave-safe bowl with the cinnamon and a tiny splash of water. Blast for 2-3 minutes until soft and jammy. Let it cool slightly.
- Add the stewed apples, milk, whey, honey, vanilla extract, and salt into a blender. Blitz on high speed until completely smooth with no lumps.
- Pour the mix into the Creami pint. Do not cross the 'Max Fill' line.
- Freeze on a perfectly flat surface for 24 hours. A slanted freeze will wreck the machine.
- Lock the frozen pint into the Creami outer bowl.
- Hit the 'Lite Ice Cream' button and let it run.
- If it looks powdery (standard for macro ice creams), add a splash of milk and hit 'Re-spin' to force the creamy texture.
- Hollow out the centre, drop in some crushed biscuits or extra diced apple, and run the 'Mix-In' program. Demolish immediately.

steps:
- Crush the digestive biscuits into fine crumbs. Melt the butter and mix thoroughly
  with the crumbs until the mixture resembles wet sand.
- Divide the crumb mixture evenly into silicone muffin liners or small air-fryer-safe
  ramekins and press down firmly. Air fry the bases at 180°C for 5-6 minutes. Remove
  and let them cool for 5-10 minutes.
- In a mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and Greek yoghurt together until completely
  smooth. Stir in the xylitol until dissolved.
- Add the egg, vanilla extract, lemon juice, and cornstarch. Whisk gently until uniform
  and velvety, being careful not to overmix.
- Pour the cheesecake batter over the cooled, pre-baked biscuit bases, filling each
  mold about 3/4 full.
- Carefully place the filled molds into the air fryer basket. Cook at 180°C for 15-17
  minutes until set. The edges should be firm, but the centers may still have a slight
  jiggle.
- Remove from the air fryer and let cool to room temperature. Transfer to the refrigerator
  and chill for at least 2 hours to set completely before serving.

faq:
- question: Does honey burn in the air fryer?
  answer: Yes, if you ignore it. Keep an eye on the last few minutes and drop the heat if it's catching.
- question: Do I need to press the tofu first?
  answer: Always. Pressing removes the excess water so it can absorb the glaze like a sponge.
steps:
- Prep the tofu by either microwaving it in 1-minute bursts until dry, or boiling it for 5-10 minutes. Removing excess water means the marinade can soak deep into the block. See our [Ultimate Quick Tofu Prep Hacks](/collections/easy-quick-tofu-prep-hacks) for the exact details.
- Whisk the honey, lemon juice, spices, water, a pinch of salt, and a splash of oil in a bowl until smooth.
- Cube the tofu and toss it through the sweet marinade until every piece is coated. Let it sit for 30 minutes if you have time.
- Air fry at 180°C for 6-10 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through. Or pan-fry in a hot pan until golden.
- Hit it with a fresh sprig of coriander (cilantro) and a wedge of lemon before serving.

- 'Warm Cheese is Mandatory: If your cream cheese is chilled straight from the fridge, it will seize up and turn your batter into a lumpy, frustrating mess.'

faq:
- question: Do I have to use erythritol?
  answer: No, use stevia, monk fruit, or standard sugar if you aren't strictly cutting.
- question: Can I bake this in a regular oven?
  answer: Absolutely. Bake at 160°C for 25-30 minutes until the edges are set but the centre still has a slight wobble.
steps:
- Whisk the erythritol, cream cheese, and egg until completely smooth. Gently fold in the yoghurt and lemon juice.
- Pour the batter into a ramekin. Bake in the air fryer at 180°C for 18-22 minutes until golden on top with a slight wobble in the centre.
- Pull it out and let it cool to room temp. Set it in the fridge for at least two hours to set completely.
